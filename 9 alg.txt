9)Write a C program that takes an integer as input and prints whether it is even or odd.




*Start the program.

*Declare an integer variable.

*Read the integer value from the user and store it in the integer variable.

*Check if the integer is divisible by 2:

*If the remainder when divided by 2 is 0, then it is even.

*Else, it is odd.

*Print whether the integer is even or odd.

*End the program.
